How to Send a
Checkout Link

Step-by-step guide for sending a checkout link to a patient

01
Go to the Dashboard
Navigate

Log in to the admin portal. You will land on the Home Dashboard.

02
Open Orders → Orders
Navigate

In the left sidebar under Management, click Orders to expand it, then select Orders from the submenu.

03
Find & Click the Patient's Name
Select

Browse the orders list and click on the patient's name (shown in blue) to open their order details.

04
Review Patient Details
Verify

Confirm you are on the correct patient's order by reviewing their name, email, phone, and address in the Patient Details panel.

05
Copy & Send the Checkout URL
Send

Scroll down to find the Checkout page URL section. Click the Copy URL button, then send the link to the patient via text, email, or direct message.

💡
Use this link when the patient did not receive the checkout email or needs to be resent the payment link to complete their order.
✅
Once the patient opens the link and completes checkout and payment went through, their order status will automatically update to Visit Pending.
✅ Checkout Link Sent!
