How to Track
an Order

Step-by-step guide for monitoring order status

01
Go to the Dashboard
Navigate

Log in to the admin portal. You will land on the Home Dashboard showing your key metrics and patient summary.

02
Open Orders → Orders
Navigate

In the left sidebar under Management, click Orders to expand it, then click Orders from the submenu.

03
Find the Patient's Order
Select

Browse the orders list to find the patient. Click on the patient's name (shown in blue) to open their order details. You can also see their order reference ID on the far right.

04
Check the Visit Status & Order Status
Track

The Visit Status column shows the current stage of the order. You can also open the Order Status dropdown to view or update the full order journey.

📋 Order Status Reference Guide
Created
Order has been created but patient has not proceeded with check out.
Processing
The order payment is being processed.
Visit Failed
The visit could not be completed. Either there is a problem with payment or the patient has not responded to the provider messages.
Visit Pending
The patient visit is scheduled with the provider.
Consult Canceled
The order was canceled before completion.
Referred
The provider has disqualified the patient.
Prescribed
A prescription has been issued for the patient.
Billing Pending
There was a problem with the brand owner's card on the billing profile. The visit will still occur, however the prescription will be put on hold and will not be sent to the pharmacy until the billing profile is updated.
Rx Sent
The prescription has been sent to the pharmacy.
Shipped
A tracking number has been generated or the medication has been shipped to the patient.
Canceled
The order has been canceled. No further action will be taken.
