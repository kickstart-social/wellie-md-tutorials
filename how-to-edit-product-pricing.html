How to Edit
Product Pricing

Step-by-step guide for updating your product prices

01
Go to the Dashboard
Navigate

Log in to the admin portal. You will land on the Home Dashboard.

02
Open Products → Products
Navigate

In the left sidebar under Tools & Services, click Products to expand it, then click Products from the submenu.

03
Find the Product to Edit
Select

Browse or search for the product you want to update. Click the ✏ pencil icon on the right side of the product row to open the edit modal.

04
Update Pricing Fields
Edit

The product editor will open. Update the fields you need under Your Pricing (Editable). Fields under Admin Costs are read-only and managed by the admin.

💲
Base Price (Patient): The retail price shown to patients. This is the main field you will update.
⚠️
Admin Costs are read-only. Cost to Client and Shipping Cost are set by the admin and cannot be changed here.
📦
About Shipping Fees: The product price you set already includes shipping and MD cost, so there is no need to add a separate shipping fee for your patients — unless you want to charge additional shipping fees on top of the product price.
05
Click Save
Done

Once you've made your changes, click the Save button to apply them. Click Cancel to discard any changes.

✅
Changes saved! The updated price will be immediately reflected for patients viewing this product.
✅ Product Pricing Updated!
