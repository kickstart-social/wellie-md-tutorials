💵 How Your Billing Works
A
Pre-Authorization on Your Card
When a patient order is processed, the pharmacy cost, shipping, and MD cost will be pre-authorized on the card saved to your billing profile.
B
Transaction Completes
Once the order transaction is complete, the full amount — including your profit margin — is settled.
C
Funds Return to Your Merchant Account
The total collected — costs plus your profit — is sent back to your merchant account. Your card is only used as a temporary hold; you keep the full revenue.
🔄 In short: Your card is pre-authorized → order ships → full amount (costs + profit) goes to your merchant account.
