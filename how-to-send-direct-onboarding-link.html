02
Find the Right Questionnaire
Select

Locate the onboarding questionnaire that matches your patient's treatment type (e.g. GLP Weight Loss, ED, NAD+, etc.). Make sure its status shows Approved.

03
Click the 🔗 Link Icon
Copy Link

In the Actions column on the right side of the row, click the chain link icon (🔗). A tooltip will appear confirming "Copy Questionnaire Link".

04
Link Copied to Clipboard!
Success

A success toast will appear confirming the link has been copied. The link format will look like this:

https://intake.kinmeds.com/visit/glpMicrodosing
05
Send the Link to Your Patient
Send

Paste and send the copied link to the patient via text, email, or direct message. When they open it, they'll land directly on the intake form for their treatment program.
